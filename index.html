Pinochle Quick Reference

Game Flow

Each round has 4 phases: Bid, Meld, Tricks, and Score.

Bidding

Players bid on how many points they think they can earn. The highest bidder names trump and must meet their bid through meld + trick points.

Going Set

If the bidder's meld + trick points don't meet their bid, they "go set" and lose points equal to their bid amount.

Winning

First player/team to reach the target score wins!
